          Use PlanMail every morning 
     to plan your inbox. 


    
          
    


         

          
     
          Planmail helps in segregating your emails in three labels: 
    
          Today : Will read/reply today 
    
          Tomorrow : Will think about this tomorrow 
    
          Someday  : I don't know, maybe weekend 

    

          To start click authorize button on the top right.  
          

    





          

    
           
    
          Security: Your emails are directly loaded in your browser. They don't even pass through our servers. We don't store anything, not even your email id. 

    

          About Permissions : View and modify but not delete your email : This permission is required to get your emails and apply labels to them. We don't edit/delete any of your labels to any email. We simply add when you tap.

          
     To access your inbox , click on the icons above when you are done segregating.
